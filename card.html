Siamese Fighting Fish

A common misconception on the Siamese fighting fish is they should live in vases or bowls, but this has been proven it damages their health and lowers their mood

  • Scientific Name: Osphronemidae
  • Average Length: 7.5cm
  • Habitat: Endemic to Thailand
  • Average Lifespan: 2 years

Siamese fighting fish usually grows to a length of about 6.5 cm (2.6 in).Although aquarium specimens are widely known for their brilliant colours and large, flowing fins, the natural coloration of B. splendens are generally dull green, brown and grey, and the fins of wild specimens are short. In captivity, they have been selectively bred to display a vibrant array of colours and tail types
